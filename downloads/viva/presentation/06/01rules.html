Established mixing rules

Level Vocal loudness should be around –3 LU relative to the total mix.
√
/
X
Median total drums loudness is between –6.5 LU and –8.5 LU .
√
Panning Lead vocals, snare drums, and low-frequency sources should be panned central.
√
Higher frequencies are panned increasingly further between 50 Hz and 400 Hz .
X
The centre of the stereo image should be sufficiently strong.
!
A mix should not be overly monaural.
!
EQ The total mix tends to a particular spectrum.
√
DRC A mix should have sufficient dynamic range: it is better to err on the side of applying (too) little compression.
√
Reverb It is better to err on the side of too little reverb, rather than too much.
√
The total reverb loudness should be about –14 LU.
X
For a higher perceived amount of reverberation, increase the reverb loudness and/or reverb time.
√
Subgrouping The number of subgroups created strongly depends on the number of tracks.
!
Tracks are typically grouped per instrument type.
!
